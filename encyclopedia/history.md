# History

Learn about past events, civilizations, and historical analysis.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Ancient History: Egypt, Greece, Rome, early civilizations
- Medieval History: Feudalism, Crusades, Islamic Golden Age
- Modern History: Renaissance, Enlightenment, Industrial Revolution
- World Wars: WWI, WWII, Cold War
- Cultural History: Art, religion, philosophy through time
- Historical Methods: Primary sources, analysis, interpretation

### Fields of Work
- **Historian**: Research and write about historical events
- **Archivist**: Preserve and organize historical documents
- **Museum Curator**: Manage collections, exhibitions
- **Archaeologist**: Excavate and study ancient artifacts
- **History Teacher/Professor**: Educate students in history
- **Historical Consultant**: Advise on films, books, documentaries
- **Genealogist**: Research family histories and lineages
