# Economics

Understand markets, finance, and economic theory.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Microeconomics: Supply and demand, market structures, elasticity
- Macroeconomics: GDP, inflation, unemployment, fiscal policy
- International Trade: Comparative advantage, tariffs, exchange rates
- Monetary Policy: Central banks, interest rates, money supply
- Development Economics: Growth, poverty, inequality
- Behavioral Economics: Decision-making, biases, psychology

### Fields of Work
- **Economist**: Analyze economic trends, policy recommendations
- **Financial Analyst**: Evaluate investments, market research
- **Investment Banker**: Advise on mergers, acquisitions, capital raising
- **Policy Advisor**: Inform government economic decisions
- **Market Research Analyst**: Study consumer behavior, market trends
- **Economic Consultant**: Provide expert analysis for businesses
- **Actuary**: Assess financial risks using economic models
