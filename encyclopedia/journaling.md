# Journaling

Guidance for personal reflection, creative writing, and maintaining a journal.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Reflective Journaling: Self-awareness, personal growth, mindfulness
- Creative Journaling: Art journals, sketches, mixed media
- Bullet Journaling: Organization, productivity, habit tracking
- Gratitude Journaling: Positive psychology, appreciation
- Dream Journaling: Recording and analyzing dreams
- Travel Journaling: Documenting experiences, adventures

### Fields of Work
- **Life Coach**: Guide clients using journaling techniques
- **Therapist**: Use journaling as therapeutic tool
- **Writer**: Develop ideas through journal writing
- **Mindfulness Instructor**: Teach reflective practices
- **Workshop Facilitator**: Lead journaling workshops, retreats
- **Content Creator**: Share journaling tips, tutorials online
- **Product Designer**: Create journals, planners, stationery
