# Mathematics

Explore algebra, geometry, calculus, statistics, and mathematical thinking.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Algebra: Equations, functions, polynomials, linear algebra
- Geometry: Shapes, proofs, trigonometry, coordinate systems
- Calculus: Derivatives, integrals, limits, differential equations
- Statistics: Probability, distributions, hypothesis testing
- Discrete Math: Combinatorics, graph theory, logic
- Number Theory: Primes, divisibility, modular arithmetic

### Fields of Work
- **Mathematician**: Research pure or applied mathematics
- **Data Analyst**: Use statistics to interpret business data
- **Actuary**: Assess financial risks for insurance companies
- **Quantitative Analyst**: Develop financial models, trading algorithms
- **Operations Research Analyst**: Optimize processes, logistics
- **Cryptographer**: Design secure encryption systems
- **Math Teacher/Professor**: Educate students in mathematics
