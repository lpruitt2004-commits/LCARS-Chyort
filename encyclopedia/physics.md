# Physics

Study the fundamental laws of nature, motion, energy, and the universe.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Classical Mechanics: Motion, forces, Newton's laws, momentum
- Thermodynamics: Heat, temperature, laws of thermodynamics
- Electromagnetism: Electric and magnetic fields, Maxwell's equations
- Quantum Mechanics: Wave-particle duality, uncertainty principle
- Relativity: Special and general relativity, space-time
- Optics: Light, lenses, reflection, refraction

### Fields of Work
- **Research Physicist**: Advance theoretical or experimental physics
- **Astrophysicist**: Study stars, galaxies, cosmology
- **Nuclear Physicist**: Research atomic nuclei, nuclear energy
- **Optical Engineer**: Design lenses, cameras, lasers
- **Geophysicist**: Study Earth's physical properties
- **Medical Physicist**: Apply physics to healthcare, radiation therapy
- **Aerospace Engineer**: Design aircraft, spacecraft systems
