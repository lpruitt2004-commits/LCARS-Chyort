# Political Science

Examine government, politics, and public policy.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Political Theory: Democracy, justice, rights, power
- Comparative Politics: Government systems, institutions worldwide
- International Relations: Diplomacy, war, global organizations
- Public Policy: Law-making, regulation, policy analysis
- Political Economy: Intersection of politics and economics
- Elections: Voting systems, campaigns, political behavior

### Fields of Work
- **Political Scientist**: Research political systems, behavior
- **Policy Analyst**: Evaluate and recommend public policies
- **Diplomat**: Represent country in international relations
- **Campaign Manager**: Organize political campaigns
- **Lobbyist**: Advocate for interests to lawmakers
- **Political Consultant**: Advise candidates, organizations
- **Intelligence Analyst**: Assess geopolitical threats
- **Government Official**: Work in public administration
