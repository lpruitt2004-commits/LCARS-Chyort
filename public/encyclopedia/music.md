# Music

Explore music theory, history, and performance.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Music Theory: Scales, chords, harmony, rhythm, notation
- Music History: Classical, jazz, rock, world music traditions
- Performance: Instruments, vocal techniques, stage presence
- Composition: Songwriting, arranging, orchestration
- Production: Recording, mixing, mastering, DAWs
- Acoustics: Sound waves, frequencies, audio engineering

### Fields of Work
- **Musician/Performer**: Play instruments, sing, perform live
- **Composer**: Write original music for films, games, concerts
- **Music Producer**: Record and produce tracks, albums
- **Music Teacher**: Educate students in music theory and performance
- **Sound Engineer**: Manage audio for recordings, live events
- **Music Therapist**: Use music for healing and rehabilitation
- **Conductor**: Lead orchestras, choirs, ensembles
- **Music Director**: Oversee musical aspects of productions
