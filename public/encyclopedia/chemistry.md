# Chemistry

Discover the building blocks of matter and chemical interactions.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Atomic Structure: Protons, neutrons, electrons, atomic theory
- Chemical Bonding: Ionic, covalent, metallic bonds
- Reactions: Synthesis, decomposition, combustion, redox
- Organic Chemistry: Carbon compounds, hydrocarbons, polymers
- Thermodynamics: Energy changes, enthalpy, entropy

### Fields of Work
- **Chemist**: Research and develop new compounds, materials
- **Pharmaceutical Scientist**: Design and test medications
- **Chemical Engineer**: Scale up chemical processes for industry
- **Forensic Chemist**: Analyze evidence for criminal investigations
- **Environmental Chemist**: Study pollution, water quality, sustainability
- **Materials Scientist**: Develop new materials, nanotechnology
- **Quality Control Analyst**: Test products for safety and quality
