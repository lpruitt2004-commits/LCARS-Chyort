# Psychology

Study the mind, behavior, and mental processes.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Cognitive Psychology: Memory, perception, problem-solving
- Developmental Psychology: Child development, lifespan changes
- Social Psychology: Group behavior, persuasion, attitudes
- Clinical Psychology: Mental disorders, therapy, treatment
- Neuropsychology: Brain-behavior relationships
- Behavioral Psychology: Conditioning, learning, reinforcement

### Fields of Work
- **Clinical Psychologist**: Diagnose and treat mental health issues
- **Counselor**: Provide therapy, support for personal challenges
- **School Psychologist**: Support students' learning and well-being
- **Industrial-Organizational Psychologist**: Improve workplace productivity
- **Research Psychologist**: Conduct studies on behavior and cognition
- **Forensic Psychologist**: Apply psychology to legal matters
- **Neuropsychologist**: Assess brain injuries and disorders
