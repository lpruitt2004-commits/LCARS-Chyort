# Art

Discover visual arts, techniques, and art history.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Drawing & Painting: Techniques, mediums, composition
- Sculpture: Modeling, carving, casting, installation
- Art History: Movements (Renaissance, Impressionism, Modernism)
- Digital Art: Graphic design, 3D modeling, animation
- Photography: Composition, lighting, post-processing
- Color Theory: Hue, saturation, value, harmony

### Fields of Work
- **Fine Artist**: Create original paintings, sculptures, installations
- **Graphic Designer**: Design logos, branding, marketing materials
- **Illustrator**: Create images for books, magazines, advertising
- **Art Director**: Oversee visual style for projects, campaigns
- **Museum Curator**: Manage art collections, exhibitions
- **Art Teacher**: Educate students in visual arts
- **Animator**: Create motion graphics, films, video games
- **Photographer**: Capture images for media, events, portraits
