# Biology

Explore the wonders of life science and living organisms.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Cell Biology: Structure and function of cells, organelles, cellular processes
- Genetics: DNA, RNA, heredity, gene expression, mutations
- Evolution: Natural selection, adaptation, speciation
- Ecology: Ecosystems, food chains, biodiversity, conservation
- Anatomy & Physiology: Human body systems, organs, tissues

### Fields of Work
- **Research Scientist**: Conduct experiments, publish findings, advance biological knowledge
- **Medical Doctor**: Diagnose and treat diseases, patient care
- **Biotechnologist**: Develop medical treatments, genetic engineering, pharmaceuticals
- **Marine Biologist**: Study ocean life, conservation, marine ecosystems
- **Microbiologist**: Research bacteria, viruses, microorganisms
- **Ecologist**: Study environmental systems, conservation efforts
- **Genetic Counselor**: Assess genetic risks, advise patients on hereditary conditions
- **Botanist**: Study plant life, agriculture, plant-based medicines
