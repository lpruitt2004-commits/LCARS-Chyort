# Geography

Study the Earth's landscapes, environments, and places.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Physical Geography: Landforms, climate, ecosystems, hydrology
- Human Geography: Population, culture, urbanization, migration
- Cartography: Map-making, GIS, spatial analysis
- Environmental Geography: Conservation, sustainability, climate change
- Economic Geography: Resource distribution, trade, development
- Geopolitics: Borders, territories, international relations

### Fields of Work
- **Geographer**: Research spatial patterns, human-environment interactions
- **Cartographer**: Create maps, spatial visualizations
- **GIS Specialist**: Analyze geographic data, mapping software
- **Urban Planner**: Design city layouts, zoning, infrastructure
- **Environmental Consultant**: Assess environmental impacts
- **Climatologist**: Study weather patterns, climate change
- **Travel Writer**: Document places, cultures, experiences
