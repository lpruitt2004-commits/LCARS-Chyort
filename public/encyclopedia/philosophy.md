# Philosophy

Examine fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, values, reason, and mind.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Metaphysics: Reality, existence, being, causation
- Epistemology: Knowledge, belief, truth, justification
- Ethics: Morality, right and wrong, virtue, duty
- Logic: Reasoning, argumentation, fallacies
- Political Philosophy: Justice, rights, government, freedom
- Philosophy of Mind: Consciousness, identity, free will

### Fields of Work
- **Philosophy Professor**: Teach and research philosophical topics
- **Ethicist**: Advise on moral issues in medicine, business
- **Policy Analyst**: Apply philosophical reasoning to public policy
- **Writer**: Explore philosophical themes in books, essays
- **Consultant**: Guide organizations on ethical decisions
- **Lawyer**: Use logic and argumentation in legal practice
- **Therapist**: Apply existential and philosophical counseling
