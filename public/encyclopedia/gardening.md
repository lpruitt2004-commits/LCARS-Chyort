# Gardening

Tips and techniques for growing plants, vegetables, and maintaining a healthy garden.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Soil Science: pH, nutrients, composting, amendments
- Plant Biology: Growth cycles, photosynthesis, propagation
- Pest Management: Organic methods, integrated pest control
- Garden Design: Layout, companion planting, aesthetics
- Vegetable Gardening: Crop rotation, harvesting, preservation
- Sustainable Practices: Water conservation, native plants, permaculture

### Fields of Work
- **Horticulturist**: Cultivate plants, manage gardens, nurseries
- **Landscape Designer**: Plan and design outdoor spaces
- **Arborist**: Care for trees, pruning, tree health
- **Urban Farmer**: Grow food in city environments
- **Greenhouse Manager**: Oversee plant production facilities
- **Gardening Consultant**: Advise homeowners, businesses on gardens
- **Botanical Garden Curator**: Manage plant collections
- **Agricultural Extension Agent**: Educate on gardening practices
