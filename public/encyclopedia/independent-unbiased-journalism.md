# Independent Unbiased Journalism

Principles and practices for ethical, unbiased reporting and investigative journalism.

## Knowledge Base

### Core Concepts
- Journalistic Ethics: Accuracy, objectivity, fairness, transparency
- Investigative Reporting: Research, sources, fact-checking
- Media Literacy: Evaluating sources, recognizing bias
- News Writing: Inverted pyramid, clarity, conciseness
- Interview Techniques: Questioning, active listening, verification
- Digital Journalism: Online platforms, multimedia storytelling

### Fields of Work
- **Investigative Journalist**: Uncover corruption, wrongdoing
- **Reporter**: Cover news events, write stories
- **Editor**: Oversee content, ensure accuracy and quality
- **Fact-Checker**: Verify information, combat misinformation
- **Photojournalist**: Document events through photography
- **Broadcast Journalist**: Report news on TV, radio
- **Data Journalist**: Analyze data for news stories
- **Independent Publisher**: Run independent news outlets
